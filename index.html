1Yonsei University   2Oracle   3Carnegie Mellon University   4University of Maryland, College Park   5University of Central Florida

Paper / Project Page Code

Abstract

Diffusion-based large language models (dLLMs) implicitly learn a mixture of semi-autoregressive experts. HEX ensembles heterogeneous block schedules and majority-votes outputs, delivering strong gains on GSM8K, MATH, ARC-C, and TruthfulQA without retraining.
